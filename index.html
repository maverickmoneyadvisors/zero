About

Services once reserved for the ulta wealthly now available to everyone.

You're an individual, your deserve individual attention. You have big plans! Your goals aren't conventional, why settle for conventional advice? You need a Maverick Advisor in your corner.



  • 1996-1998

    Our Humble Beginnings

    I started out in an entirely unrealted business. One day I was talking with another entrepreneur and providing some advice and coaching. At the end of a long meeting they thanked me and asked how much they owed for my time and advice. I had been providing advice for free, becasue I enjoyed helping achieve their dreams. Turns out I should have paid him because it helped me to understand that entrepreneurs needed and and would pay for down to earth, hands on advice from a fellow entrepreneur.

  • 2000-2015

    A Multi Familly Office is Born

    We became a Multi Family Office. Helping families of business owners with very complex situations across North America.

  • 2013

    Transition to serve a broader audience

    Demand from friends, familly and other mass market clients started to really become obvious. While their needs are just as important and complex, the challenge was that they could not afford the same levels of service. By the end of this year I started the exit process for the old way determined to find a way to bring the same comprehensive service, formerly available to only the very wealthy, to everyone.

  • 2016

    Research and Development

    It is clear that leveraging technology would be the driver for supporting our mission of having everyone Take Control of YOUR Financial Future! We have spent the last 3 years researching security, integration with other financial services, and systematizing our in person processes to be delivered at a distance. We are there now! We are now building an application to deliver this all to you. Sign up to be among the first to get get access and we will keep you posted on our progress! We expect to launch early 2017.
